Membership

Join our thriving cooperative community and enjoy exclusive benefits designed to support your financial growth and stability.

Benefits of Membership

  • Access to low-interest loans
  • Competitive savings interest rates
  • Distribution of interest and share capital
  • Financial education and workshops
  • Death benefits assistance
  • Community support programs

Requirements

  • Must be a Filipino Citizen
  • Must be at least 18 years old
  • Valid government-issued ID
  • Proof of residence (utility bill)
  • 2x2 recent ID photo
  • Must subscribe to at least 120 shares
  • Initial share capital contribution
  • Pre-membership seminar certificate

How to Become a Member

Follow these simple steps to join our cooperative community

1

Attend Online Pre-Membership Education Seminar

Our pre-membership education seminar will help you understand the benefits, rights, and responsibilities of being a cooperative member.

Watch the entire video to fully understand cooperative principles and how Talcreco Talibon operates.
2

Take the Assessment

After watching the seminar video, test your understanding by completing our membership assessment form.

Take Assessment Now
You must score at least 80% to pass the assessment.
  • The assessment consists of 20 multiple-choice questions
  • You have 30 minutes to complete the assessment
  • You can retake the assessment if you don't pass on your first attempt
3

Receive Your Certificate

Upon successfully passing the assessment, you will receive a pre-membership certificate via email.

Your certificate will be sent within 24 hours of passing the assessment.

If you don't pass the assessment:

  • Watch the video again
  • You can retake the assessment anytime
  • There is no limit on the number of attempts
4

Complete Your Application

Visit our office with your pre-membership certificate and required documents to finalize your membership.

Required Documents:
  • Pre-membership certificate
  • Valid government-issued ID
  • Proof of residence
  • 2x2 ID photo
  • Membership application form (provided at the office)
Initial Payment:
  • Membership fee: ₱150
  • Minimum share capital: ₱3,000
  • Savings deposit: ₱1,000
5

Welcome to Talcreco Talibon!

Congratulations! You are now officially a member of Talcreco Talibon Credit Cooperative.

Your membership ID and passbook will be issued immediately.
