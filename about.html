Our Story

For many years, small market vendors in Talibon Public Market struggled with capital funding, often resorting to usurers and high-interest loans that significantly reduced their earnings. With vending being the livelihood of more than 1,000 families, providing an alternative credit facility became imperative.

In 1990, DTI-Bohol personnel challenged the Municipal Development Council to organize a cooperative that would serve as a medium for social progress. As business activities in Talibon Public Market were rapidly increasing, small businesses in need of capitalization were at the mercy of predatory lenders.

In response to this need, a group of committed civic leaders banded together in 1991 to form the TALIBON CREDIT COOPERATIVE (TALCRECO). Their vision was to create an excellent medium of service by pooling members' resources, introducing capital mobilization programs, and providing financial assistance to members in need, liberating them from the control of usurers.

1991

TALCRECO was founded with 27 members and a total share capital contribution of ₱30,000.

Early Years

Like any fledgling cooperative, TALCRECO experienced financial difficulty in its decisive years but endured the test of time with the blessing of the Holy Trinity.

SEAD Partnership

Small Economic Activity Development (formerly CARE PHILS.) provided crucial financial and technical assistance during our most troubled times. The Member Savings Operation (MSO) proved valuable to our cooperative operation.

Policy Reforms

The Board of Directors instituted policy reforms that strengthened operations in compliance with standard business practices and aggressively pursued members' education through cooperative-sponsored seminars.

Land Bank Partnership

As business opportunities expanded requiring more capital funding, Land Bank provided us with needed capital and technical assistance to support our growth.

July 2015

The dream of a cooperative home office building became reality with the completion of the TALCRECO Building, a proud testament of members' endless support and dynamic cooperative strength.

TALCRECO Today

From humble beginnings, we have grown into a thriving cooperative serving our community.
